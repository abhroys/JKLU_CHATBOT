
    Q: What scholarships are available for the BBA program at JKLU?
    A: Scholarships for the BBA program are awarded based on academic performance in Class XII or CUET scores. Special category scholarships, including women's scholarships and Early Bird Scholarships, are also available.

    Q: How are the scholarships for the BBA program at JKLU structured?
    A: Scholarships range from 15% to 100% based on academic performance, with specific scholarships for the dependents of JKLU staff, alumni, and those demonstrating exceptional achievements.
