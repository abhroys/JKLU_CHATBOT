
    Q: What is the objective of the Council of Technical Affairs at JKLU?
    A: The Council of Technical Affairs is responsible for the university's technological infrastructure, ensuring its dependability and offering solutions to enhance the academic and institutional experience.

    Q: What are the responsibilities of the Council of Technical Affairs at JKLU?
    A:
    1. Maintain communication on technology-related matters with the Department of Student Affairs.
    2. Improve technological infrastructure and assist students in launching campus-wide technology initiatives.
    3. Explore technology's role in reducing JKLU's carbon footprint and improving environmental performance.
