
    Q: What utility services are available at JKLU?
    A: JKLU provides an on-campus laundry service at ‘Mr. Hi’ Haier wash facility, available six days a week, and a free shuttle bus service connecting the campus with the city of Jaipur.

    Q: What banking facilities are available near JKLU?
    A: The IDBI Bank branch near JKLU offers students the ability to open accounts and access education loans. The bank operates from 10 am to 4 pm.
