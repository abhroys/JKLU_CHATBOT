
    Q: Who is Dr Mahesh Kumar Yadav at JKLU?
    A: Dr Mahesh Kumar Yadav is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at JKLU. He holds a PhD from IIT Kanpur, where his research focused on nuclear reactor containment cooling. He has worked extensively on hydrogen management and thermo-fluidic systems.

    Q: What are Dr Mahesh Kumar Yadav's research interests?
    A: His research focuses on hydrogen management, phase-change heat transfer, and renewable energy systems.
