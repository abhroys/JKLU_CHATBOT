
    Q: What is the objective of the Council of Campus Life at JKLU?
    A: The Council of Campus Life ensures that JKLU is welcoming and enjoyable for everyone, by improving campus facilities, student housing, dining, and transportation.

    Q: What are the responsibilities of the Council of Campus Life at JKLU?
    A:
    1. Ensure communication between students and the Department of Student Affairs regarding housing, dining, and transportation.
    2. Work on resolving student complaints about the overall campus experience and compile suggestions for improvements.
