
    Q: What is the objective of the Council of Academic Affairs at JKLU?
    A: The Council of Academic Affairs serves as a liaison between students and the administration to create a productive academic environment, supporting student concerns and collaborating with academic offices.

    Q: What are the responsibilities of the Council of Academic Affairs at JKLU?
    A:
    1. Boost infrastructure and services in the libraries.
    2. Assist in the resolution of academic complaints and improve communication with the Office of Academic Affairs (OAA).
    3. Organize discussions between students and faculty on academic matters.
