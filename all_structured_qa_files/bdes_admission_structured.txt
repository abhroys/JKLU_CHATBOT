
    Q: What is the structure of the B.Des. programme admission at JKLU?
    A: The programme has a common foundation year that introduces critical thinking, design drawing, material manipulation, communication, and leadership, with discipline selection in the subsequent years.

    Q: What are the disciplines available after the foundation year in B.Des. at JKLU?
    A:
    1. Interdisciplinary Design
    2. Interaction Design
    3. Product Design
    4. Integrated Communication Design
