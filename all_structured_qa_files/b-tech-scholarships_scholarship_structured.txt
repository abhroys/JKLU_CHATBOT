
    Q: What scholarships are available for the B.Tech program at JKLU?
    A: Scholarships for the B.Tech program are awarded based on academic performance in Class XII, JEE Mains scores, or CUET performance. Scholarships range from 15% to 100%, with special provisions for women, defense personnel, and other categories.

    Q: What are the performance-based scholarship renewal criteria for B.Tech students at JKLU?
    A: Scholarships for subsequent years are based on CGPA performance, with students achieving 9.5 or above eligible for 100% scholarship, and scaling down to 15% for a CGPA of 7.5 to 8.0.
