
    Q: What is the objective of the B.Des. in Product Design at JKLU?
    A: The programme aims to create individualistic product designers who are skilled in both hands-on creation and leveraging new-age technology to create sustainable, people-centric products.

    Q: What are the career prospects for B.Des. in Product Design graduates?
    A: Graduates find roles in the manufacturing sector, automobile industry, consumer goods, industrial products, and can also become entrepreneurs or innovators in product design.

    Q: What is taught in the B.Des. Product Design programme?
    A: Courses focus on developing products for diverse sectors like agriculture, medical, children's products, and digital products with an emphasis on sustainability.
