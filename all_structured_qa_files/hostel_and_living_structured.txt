
    Q: What accommodation facilities are available at JKLU?
    A: JKLU offers separate hostels for boys and girls with fully furnished single, double, and triple occupancy rooms. Common areas with indoor games, TV lounges, and seating arrangements promote social interaction and relaxation.

    Q: What dining options are available on the JKLU campus?
    A: JKLU provides a hygienic and nutritious dining experience in the mess hall, and students can enjoy snacks at the multi-cuisine cafeteria, which offers a variety of options for every palate.
