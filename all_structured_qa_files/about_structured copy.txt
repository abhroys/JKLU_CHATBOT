
    Q: What is the B.Tech program at JKLU's Institute of Engineering and Technology?
    A: The B.Tech program at JKLU is designed to align with the latest technological advancements, offering project-based learning and interdisciplinary curriculum. It prepares students for future careers or academic research.

    Q: What are the highlights of the B.Tech program at JKLU?
    A: The program offers short-term courses on cutting-edge technologies, with opportunities for students to pursue a semester at IITs, IIITs, and foreign institutions, providing global exposure.

    Q: What is the teaching methodology for B.Tech at JKLU?
    A: The curriculum is project-based with frameworks for effective learning, fostering free exchange of ideas between students and teachers, and enhancing problem-solving skills.
