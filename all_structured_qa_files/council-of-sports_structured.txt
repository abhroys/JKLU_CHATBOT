
    Q: What is the objective of the Council of Sports at JKLU?
    A: The Sports Council promotes a healthy sporting environment on campus, maintaining sports facilities, and encouraging student participation in both leisure and competitive sports.

    Q: What are the responsibilities of the Council of Sports at JKLU?
    A:
    1. Advocate for appropriate sports facilities and ensure their regular maintenance.
    2. Encourage team spirit and organize effective sports events, promoting a healthy competitive atmosphere.
    3. Support student athletes and promote health, wellness, and fitness among the student body.
