
    Q: What are the Centres of Excellence at JKLU?
    A: The Centres of Excellence at JKLU integrate innovation and research into student life. These centres focus on interdisciplinary learning, student-centric pedagogy, and fostering industry connections for comprehensive development.

    Q: How do the Centres of Excellence enhance the learning experience at JKLU?
    A: The Centres provide an interactive learning environment, offering students opportunities for research, innovation, industry exposure, and international collaboration.
