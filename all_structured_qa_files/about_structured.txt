
    Q: What is the objective of the BBA Programme at JKLU?
    A: The BBA programme at JKLU provides learners a strong foundation in core management, new-age skills like communications, critical thinking, and entrepreneurial mindset. It ensures job-readiness through focused specializations.

    Q: What specializations are available in the BBA Programme at JKLU?
    A:
    1. BBA Banking & Fintech: Focuses on new-age banking and fintech, with industry partnerships and internships.
    2. BBA Entrepreneurship: Offers hands-on experience in starting and managing businesses with access to Atal Incubation Centre and a Start-up Studio.
    3. BBA Interdisciplinary: Combines core management with courses in technology and design, offering flexible specialization options.
