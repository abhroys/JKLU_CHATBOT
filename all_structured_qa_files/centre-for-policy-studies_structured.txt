
    Q: What is the Centre for Policy Studies at JKLU?
    A: The Centre for Policy Studies is a think-tank at JKLU that provides thought leadership to inform policymaking in India. It conducts research, hosts events, and brings together scholars and researchers to address critical policy issues.

    Q: Who leads the Centre for Policy Studies at JKLU?
    A: The Centre is chaired by Mr. Ajay Shankar, former Secretary to the Government of India, and includes prominent scholars like Prof. Alasdair Roberts and Prof. Ray La Raja from UMass Amherst.
