
    Q: What are the key research areas at JKLU?
    A: JKLU focuses on trans-disciplinary innovation and industrial research. The university emphasizes applied research in key areas such as data science, sustainable development, robotics, AI, and IoT.

    Q: What is JKLU's contribution to industry research?
    A: JKLU has partnered with industry leaders like JK Organisation, CEERI Pilani, and ISRO to work on projects such as automation for tyre manufacturing, satellite image registering automation, and alternative heat transfer fluids.
