
    Q: What is the Centre for Communication and Critical Thinking (CCCT) at JKLU?
    A: CCCT promotes personal and professional growth by focusing on communication, critical thinking, problem-solving, and negotiation skills. The Centre uses active learning approaches through communication labs, events, and core courses for students.

    Q: What unique learning pathways are offered by CCCT at JKLU?
    A: CCCT offers six unique courses, including public speaking, negotiation, and decision making, alongside 10-12 hours of Communication Laboratory sessions, where students practice thinking, writing, and speaking.
