
    Q: What is the purpose of IQAC at JKLU?
    A: The Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC) is responsible for planning and monitoring activities to enhance the quality of the teaching-learning process and research at JKLU, ensuring high standards in every domain.

    Q: How does IQAC ensure quality at JKLU?
    A: IQAC implements processes to bring quality in 'input, processes, and output' across teaching, learning, and research activities.
