
    Q: What is JK Lakshmipat University (JKLU)?
    A: JKLU, established in 2011 by the J.K. Organisation, offers cutting-edge programs in engineering, management, and design, with a focus on bridging the industry-academia gap through experiential learning, internships, and personalized guidance.

    Q: What is JKLU's vision and mission?
    A: JKLU's vision is to be one of India's most innovative higher education institutions. Its mission includes fostering critical thinking, problem-solving, interdisciplinary learning, and research that leads to real-world applications.
