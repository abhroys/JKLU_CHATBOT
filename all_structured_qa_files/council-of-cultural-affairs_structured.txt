
    Q: What is the objective of the Council of Cultural Affairs at JKLU?
    A: The Council of Cultural Affairs seeks to maximize the value of the cultural component of student life by organizing a diverse range of activities, while promoting collaborations with universities, colleges, and artists to enhance on-campus events.

    Q: What are the responsibilities of the Council of Cultural Affairs at JKLU?
    A:
    1. Promote student involvement in cultural events and community service.
    2. Increase campus event frequency, collaborate with clubs, and encourage intercollegiate participation.
    3. Enable calendar planning and ensure dialogue between students and the Department of Student Affairs.
