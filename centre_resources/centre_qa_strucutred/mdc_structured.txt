
    Q: What is the Management Development Centre (MDC) at JKLU?
    A: MDC at JKLU conducts management workshops and training sessions aimed at enhancing staff skills and acquiring new-age competencies. The Centre has state-of-the-art facilities with guest accommodation for participants.

    Q: What facilities does the Management Development Centre offer at JKLU?
    A: MDC provides two training rooms, a guest house with double and single occupancy rooms, and a fully functional kitchen for participants and guests.
