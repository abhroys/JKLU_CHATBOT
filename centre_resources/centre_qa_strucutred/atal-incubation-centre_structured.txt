
    Q: What is the Atal Incubation Centre (AIC) at JKLU?
    A: The Atal Incubation Centre at JKLU, in collaboration with Atal Innovation Mission, NITI Aayog, promotes innovation and entrepreneurship. AIC supports start-ups by providing seed funding, mentorship, and access to world-class labs and technical infrastructure.

    Q: What ventures have been supported by the Atal Incubation Centre?
    A: Two ventures supported by AIC are "Act For Future" (focused on education and environmental welfare) and "Sizot" (a branding and marketing agency).
