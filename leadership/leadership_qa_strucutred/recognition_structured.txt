
    Q: What is the legal recognition of JK Lakshmipat University?
    A: JKLU was established through "The JK Lakshmipat University, Jaipur Act, 2011" by the State Legislature of Rajasthan and is covered under section 2(f) of the UGC Act, 1956.

    Q: Is JKLU recognized for research and industrial contributions?
    A: Yes, JKLU is recognized as a Scientific and Industrial Research Organisation (SIRO) by the Department of Scientific and Industrial Research, Ministry of Science and Technology, Government of India.
