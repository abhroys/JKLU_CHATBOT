
    Q: What is the role of the Academic Council at JKLU?
    A: The Academic Council is the principal academic body that supervises the university's academic policies, working closely with faculty from JKLU's Institutes of Engineering, Management, and Design, along with scholars from top institutions.

    Q: Who are some of the members of the Academic Council at JKLU?
    A:
    1. Dr. Dheeraj Sanghi, Vice Chancellor, JKLU.
    2. Dr. Alka Mahajan, Pro Vice Chancellor, JKLU.
    3. Prof. A. Balasubramaniam, Director, Institute of Design, JKLU.
