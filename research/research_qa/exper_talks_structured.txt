
    Q: What expert talks have been delivered as part of JKLU's Distinguished Lecture Series?
    A: JKLU has hosted talks on diverse topics such as strategic leadership by Lt. Gen Alok Singh Kler, AI applied in manufacturing by Alessandro Liani, and systems engineering by Dr. Janet Allen from the University of Oklahoma.

    Q: How does JKLU encourage industry-academic interaction through expert talks?
    A: JKLU has invited experts from academia and industry for lectures, including topics like autonomous e-mobility, cybersecurity, and research navigation during the pandemic.
