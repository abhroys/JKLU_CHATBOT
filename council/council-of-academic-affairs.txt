Student Council
Council of Academic Affairs

Objective
The Council of Academic Affairs seeks to serve as a liaison between students and the administration and to support students' participation in a productive and easygoing academic environment. The council envisions to bring the student-centric concerns to the forefront while collaborating closely with various administrative offices and academic divisions to achieve a mutually beneficial ecosystem on campus.

Roles and Responsibilities
Boost infrastructure and services in the libraries.
Ensure smooth access and communication between the OAA and the student body.
Assist in timely and effective resolution of academic complaints.
Obtain feedback and suggestions from the students on their academic experience and progress.
Encourage constructive discussions between students and faculty.
Organise and participate in periodic meetings with the Department of Student Affairs and furnish the MOM to all participants.
