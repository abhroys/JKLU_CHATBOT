
    Q: What is the purpose of the Student Council at JKLU?
    A: The Student Council works closely with the Department of Student Affairs to advocate for student welfare, oversee campus events, and serve as a platform for students to express their concerns and ideas.

    Q: What is the composition of the JKLU Student Council?
    A: The council includes 56 members from various institutions and batches, with nearly equal representation of boys and girls, led by a President and General Secretaries across six sub-councils.
