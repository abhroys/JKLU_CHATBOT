Council of Sports

Objective
The Sports Council seeks to promote a healthy sporting environment on campus, maintain the sports facilities, and increase student involvement in leisure and competitive sports.

Roles and Responsibilities
Ensure prompt communication to the Department of Student Affairs on issues pertaining to sports and actions taken thereon.
Advocate for appropriate sports facilities and ensure regular maintenance of the existing ones on campus.
Offer assistance to athletes representing JKLU.
Encourage team spirit among students and a healthy competitive atmosphere on campus.
Strive to make sports events more effectively organised.
Bridge the gap between academics and athletics.
Encourage health, wellness, and fitness awareness among students.
Encourage sports persons through acknowledgement and appreciation of their accomplishments.
