
    Q: What is the objective of the BBA in Entrepreneurship at JKLU?
    A: The programme helps students develop entrepreneurial skills to start new businesses or grow existing ones through hands-on experience and expert guidance.

    Q: What are the key highlights of the BBA in Entrepreneurship?
    A:
    1. Project-driven Internships with start-ups and incubation centers.
    2. Start-up Studio to pilot business ideas.
    3. Multidisciplinary curriculum with industry-relevant skills.

    Q: What is the curriculum for BBA in Entrepreneurship?
    A: 
    - Semester 1: Business Management, Economics, Accounting, etc.
    - Semester 2: Business Mathematics, Marketing, Capstone projects, etc.
