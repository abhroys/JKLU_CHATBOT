
    Q: What is the objective of the BBA in Interdisciplinary at JKLU?
    A: The programme offers a customisable degree that blends core management skills with technology applications and design, allowing students to specialize in areas like marketing, finance, or data analytics.

    Q: What are the key highlights of the BBA Interdisciplinary programme?
    A:
    1. Tech Product Development in the sixth semester.
    2. Flexibility in course selection.
    3. Multidisciplinary curriculum with real-life industry exposure.

    Q: What is the curriculum for BBA in Interdisciplinary?
    A: 
    - Semester 1: Business Management, Economics, Accounting, etc.
    - Semester 2: Business Mathematics, Marketing, Design Thinking, etc.
