
    Q: How does the admission process at JKLU work?
    A:
    1. Submission of the application form.
    2. Personal Interaction Round with faculty (if eligibility criteria are met).
    3. Merit-based assessment based on attitude and aptitude.

    Q: What is the eligibility criteria for admission at JKLU?
    A: A minimum of 60% aggregate marks in Class XII in English and three subjects. Students in their final year may apply, but admission will be provisional.

    Q: What is the fee structure for the BBA programme at JKLU?
    A: 
    - Academic Fee: ₹1,52,000
    - Admission Fee: ₹5,000
    - Caution Deposit (Refundable): ₹10,000
    Total: ₹1,67,000
