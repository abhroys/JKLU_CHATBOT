
    Q: What is the objective of the BBA in Banking and Finance at JKLU?
    A: The programme prepares students to work at the intersection of finance and technology with an industry-integrated curriculum and real-life exposure through internships.

    Q: What are the key highlights of the BBA Banking and Finance programme?
    A:
    1. Project-driven Internships
    2. Industry-integrated learning
    3. Multidisciplinary opportunities with exposure to business, design, and technology.

    Q: What is the curriculum for BBA Banking and Finance?
    A: 
    - Semester 1: Foundations of Business Management, Economics-I, Accounting, Entrepreneurship, etc.
    - Semester 2: Business Mathematics, Marketing, Financial Services and Fintech, etc.
