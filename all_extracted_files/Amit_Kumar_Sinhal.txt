Faculty Name: Amit Kumar Sinhal

--- Education ---
• Post Doctoral from University of Louisiana at Lafayette, USA• M.Tech. and PhD (Computer Science and Engineering) from Rajeev Gandhi Technical University, Bhopal (M.P.), India.• B.E. (Computer Engineering) from Sardar Vallabhbhai National Institute of Technology (SVNIT), Surat, India.

--- Experience ---
Amit Kumar Sinhal is Professor of Computer Science and Engineering at the Institute of Engineering and Technology, JKLU. An alumnus of Sardar Vallabhbhai National Institute of Technology (SVNIT), Surat and Rajeev Gandhi Technical University, Bhopal;  Sinhal has more than two decades of experience in Research, Teaching and IT Industry.He has worked in Atlanta, USA for an on-site project of CoreCard Inc. His research interest includes Software Engineering, Soft Computing, AI, ML and NLP. He has filed for two patents and is currently serving as Editor-in-chief of Scopus Indexed International Journal IJETAE. He has four book Chapters and one book to his credit. He has organised many international conferences and FDPs and published many research papers in international journals.
