Student Council
Council of Campus Life

Objective
To make JKLU welcoming and enjoyable for everyone while working to ensure that all student communities on campus have access to a better and more lively campus.

Roles and Responsibilities
Ensure communication between the Department of Student Affairs and the student community on matters of housing, dining, and transportation.
Assist in timely resolution of student complaints concerning the overall college experience.
Compile comments and suggestions in this matter for appropriate action and effective resolution.
Encourage constructive dialogue between various student communities on campus.
Work together to ensure better standards of the facilities and services offered on campus.

Participate in routine meetings with the Department of Student Affairs and distribute the MOM to all participants.
