--- Experience ---
Dr. Arpan Gupta is Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science at the Institute of Engineering and Technology, JKLU. He completed his B.Tech. in Information Technology from SRMCEM, Lucknow followed by M.Tech. In CSE from LNMIIT, Jaipur.He obtained his PhD in Computer Science and Engineering from LNMIIT, Jaipur. Prior to joining JKLU, he also worked as a Senior Data Scientist at Algo8 AI Pvt. Ltd. His research interest lies in Computer Vision and Deep Learning, especially in activity understanding in sports videos.
