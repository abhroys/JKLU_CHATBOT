

# The Muse-Ink (Literary Club) #


# About the club #

The Muse-Ink Literary Club is where the mundane turns magical and the alphabets create symphonies of prose and poetry. It is a testament to the power of storytelling, the beauty of language and the endless possibilities of the written word.

Every week, the club transforms into a cauldron of ideas and voices, where debates, discussions, and story-writing sessions and competitions challenge the imagination. The club also organises regular book reading sessions and reviews that have become a staple for the members. Deep conversations about distant galaxies and bygone eras infuse newfound energy and curiosity in the young minds and present fresh insights into the world around us. Join us and let your imagination take wings.


# Our Mission #

The Musk Ink Literary Club aims to offer students a perspective on political and global affairs through regular debates and discussions at the inter-college and national level while providing a fun way to develop their oratory skills. The club also organises several other activities such as group discussions, literature quiz, etc to promote and support a fun learning environment for the club members.


# What We Offer #
- To develop different perspectives of appreciation for literature in the students.
- To encourage creative writing and expression among the students community.
- To provide a platform for debating events.

# Common Rules and regulations: #

- All club members are advised to attend all the club’s meetings

# Club Rules and Regulations #
- The club members may suggest a new activity or take part in/host an existing activity.
- All club members must agree to attend club meetings.
- Evaluation must be conducted in an appropriate manner.
- Even in on-campus mode, the contest will be done online.
- Only the teams who meet the eligibility criteria will be registered and permitted to take part in any competition.
- No team or individual participant in more than one team may register twice.
