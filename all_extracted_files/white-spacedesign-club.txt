

# White Space (Design Club) #


# About the Club #

White Space denotes the blank canvas where creativity knows no bounds. It is a dynamic hub of creative minds dedicated to spreading the essence of design thinking across diverse academic streams. In this open space, design is a tool that fuels innovation by providing a creative environment.

Our purpose is rooted in the belief that design is not limited to a single domain but can be a universal language of innovation and problem-solving. White Space is a platform that thrives on the enthusiasm and creativity of its members.


# Our Mission #

At White Space, our primary mission is to introduce the world of design to students across various academic backgrounds. We aim to make design thinking accessible, engaging, and enjoyable. Our goal is to create a collaborative and inclusive platform for everyone to meet new people, ignite their creative spark, and explore innovative solutions through design.


# What We Offer #

– Inclusive Learning

At White Space, we believe that design is not limited by academic boundaries. We encourage students from diverse streams to join us, fostering an environment where everyone’s unique perspectives contribute to our creative journey.

– Creative Workshops

We offer exciting and interactive workshops that teach design thinking principles in a fun and engaging way. These workshops equip participants with problem-solving skills that extend beyond the realm of design.

– Inspiring Competitions

White Space hosts thrilling design competitions that challenge participants to think outside the box and apply design thinking to real-world problems. These events provide a platform for individuals to showcase their creativity and innovation.

– Community Building

Beyond design projects and workshops, White Space is about building lasting connections. As a member, you’ll have the opportunity to collaborate with peers, mentors, and professionals, expanding your network and enriching your college experience.

# Club Rules and Regulations #
- The club members may suggest a new activity or take part in/host an existing activity.
- All club members must agree to attend club meetings.
- Evaluation must be conducted in an appropriate manner.
- Even in on-campus mode, the contest will be done online.
- Only the teams who meet the eligibility criteria will be registered and permitted to take part in any competition.
- No team or individual participant in more than one team may register twice.
- During the contest term, each participating team must submit solutions.
- Sharing duplicate submissions and codes is prohibited, and any such incident will result in disqualification.
