

# Sociama (Media Club) #


# About the club #

Sociama, JKLU’s media club is a student organisation dedicated to exploring various aspects of mass media, including journalism, blogs, short films, and digital communication. It serves as a platform for students interested in media-related fields to collaborate, create, and engage with media content. Activities include publishing university newsletters, organising film screenings, and hosting discussions on media ethics and trends. Media club also provides valuable hands-on experience, networking opportunities, and a space for creative expression, where students develop essential skills for future careers in media, journalism, and communication industry and foster a deeper understanding of media’s role in society.


# Mission #
- To bridge the gap between student activities and college activities.
- To help the students unleash their potential and enhance their passion in media related fields.
- To help them develop leadership abilities.
- To encourage student engagement and innovation.
- To inspire students to work in groups.


# What we offer #

By giving students the chance to express themselves through media and communication, we hope to become the most interesting and innovative club at JKLU.

# Club Rules and Regulations #
- The club members may suggest a new activity or take part in/host an existing activity.
- All club members must agree to attend club meetings.
- Evaluation must be conducted in an appropriate manner.
- Even in on-campus mode, the contest will be done online.
- Only the teams who meet the eligibility criteria will be registered and permitted to take part in any competition.
- No team or individual participant in more than one team may register twice.
- During the contest term, each participating team must submit solutions.
- Sharing duplicate submissions and codes is prohibited, and any such incident will result in disqualification.
