Faculty Name: Dr Rabia Kamra

--- Education ---
PhD from IIT Delhi.M.Sc. Hons. (Mathematics) from Punjab University, Chandigarh.

--- Experience ---
Dr. Kamra is Assistant Professor in the Department of Science and Liberal Arts at the Institute of Engineering and Technology, JKLU. She obtained her PhD from Indian Institute of Technology Delhi in 2017, under the supervision of Prof. S. Chandra Sekhara Rao. Her research work is primarily focused on designing parallel algorithms for large linear systems with special structures and properties. She has published several research papers in journals of international repute. Before joining JKLU, She worked as Assistant Professor at Punjab Engineering College (Deemed to be University), Chandigarh and NIT Delhi.
