--- Experience ---
Dr. Monika Soni is Assistant Professor at the Institute of Management, JKLU. She is a NET-JRF (Management) and has completed her PhD from Mohanlal Sukhadia University in carbon management area in 2018. She has completed an online faculty development program from Indian Institute of Management, Ahmedabad in 2021. She has rich postdoctoral experience of research and teaching undergraduate and postgraduate students of business management. She has worked as a Research Associate and Guest Faculty in Mohanlal Sukhadia University. She has also worked as a Teaching Associate in Indian Institute of Management (OB/HR), Udaipur.
