--- Experience ---
Devendra Bhavsar is Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science Engineering. He completed his M. Tech. in Computer Science & Engineering from IIT, Bombay and B.E. in Computer Science from Ujjain Engineering College, Ujjain. He has almost 11 years of academic and research experience. He is currently pursuing his PhD in the area of Big Data Analytics. He is a CISCO Certified Academy Instructor (CCAI).He has published several research papers in refereed international and national journals (ESCI/ Scopus indexed/ Web of science/ UGC indexed), and conference proceedings. During his academic career, he has held various administrative positions such as Academic Coordinator IT, CISCO Networking Academy In-Charge, Chief Warden, member of Anti-ragging Committee, member of Library Committee, Sports Club In-charge to name a few. He has been appointed as paper setter and external examiner for the viva-voce exams of B.Tech./ M.Tech. students at various universities. Prior to joining JK Lakshmipat University, he was associated with Govt. Ujjain Engineering College Ujjain and ICFAI University, Dehradun.
