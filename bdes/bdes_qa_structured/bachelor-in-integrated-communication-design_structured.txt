
    Q: What is the objective of the B.Des. in Integrated Communication Design at JKLU?
    A: The programme prepares students for careers in communication design through hands-on, project-based learning, with a focus on digital and traditional media.

    Q: What are the career prospects for B.Des. in Integrated Communication Design graduates?
    A: Graduates can work in graphic design firms, tech companies, film and video studios, startups, NGOs, or start their own design enterprises.

    Q: What is taught in the B.Des. Integrated Communication Design programme?
    A: Courses cover graphic design, web design, information architecture, advertising, social communication, and motion graphics, with a focus on human-centric communication projects.
