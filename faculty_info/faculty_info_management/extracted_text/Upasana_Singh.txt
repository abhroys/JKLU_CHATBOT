Faculty Name: Upasana Singh

--- Education ---

Certification in HR Analytics from IIM, Rohtak.
Biz Lab Trainer from All India Management Association.

--- Experience ---
Dr. Upasana Singh is Associate Professor at the Institute of Management, JKLU. She has 13 years of experience in teaching, research, and training. She is passionate about nurturing thoughts, igniting minds, and building capabilities. She firmly believes that “education is empowerment” and enjoys her role as an educationist.
