
    Q: Who is Dr Jaya Gupta at JKLU?
    A: Dr Jaya Gupta is an Associate Professor at the Institute of Engineering & Technology, JKLU. She holds a PhD from the University of Rajasthan and specializes in statistical distribution theory, fractional calculus, and integral transforms.

    Q: What are Dr Jaya Gupta's research interests?
    A: Her research focuses on statistical distribution theory, fractional calculus, and special functions.
