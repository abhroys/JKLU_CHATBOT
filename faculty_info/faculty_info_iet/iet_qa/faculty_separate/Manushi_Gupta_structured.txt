
    Q: Who is Dr Manushi Gupta at JKLU?
    A: Dr Manushi Gupta is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Science and Liberal Arts at JKLU. She holds a PhD from IIT (BHU), Varanasi, and specializes in mathematical modeling on thermoelastic problems.

    Q: What are Dr Manushi Gupta's research interests?
    A: Her research interests include mathematical modeling on non-conventional thermoelastic problems.
