
    Q: Who is Prof. Jyoti Prakash Cr Naidu at JKLU?
    A: Prof. Jyoti Prakash Naidu is the Dean (R&D) at JK Lakshmipat University, with more than three decades of experience in industrial R&D and academia. He has worked internationally, contributing to institutions in the UK, USA, Canada, and India. His research interests include Design Theory and Methodology, robotics, automation, and intelligent systems.

    Q: What are Prof. Jyoti Prakash Naidu's teaching interests?
    A: His teaching interests include Design for Patentability, Design for Sustainability, Design for Automation, and Robotics.
