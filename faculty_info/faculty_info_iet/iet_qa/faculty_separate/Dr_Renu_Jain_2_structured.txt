
    Q: Who is Dr Renu Jain at JKLU?
    A: Dr Renu Jain is the Dean and Officiating Director of the Institute of Engineering and Technology at JKLU. She holds a PhD from BITS Pilani and has over 31 years of experience in research, teaching, and administration in prestigious institutions like IIT Delhi and BITS Pilani.

    Q: What are Dr Renu Jain's research interests?
    A: Her research interests include computer-based information systems and software technology.
