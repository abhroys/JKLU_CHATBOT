
    Q: Who is Dr Mohd Shahnawaz Khan at JKLU?
    A: Dr Mohd Shahnawaz Khan is an Associate Professor in the Department of Chemistry at JKLU. He holds a PhD in Synthetic Organic and Medicinal Chemistry and has published extensively in his field.

    Q: What are Dr Mohd Shahnawaz Khan's research interests?
    A: His research focuses on green chemistry, bioactive heterocyclic compounds, and waste-to-energy initiatives.
