--- Experience ---
Dr. Ridhma is Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Institute of Engineering and Technology, JKLU. She completed her B.E. in Computer Science in 2015 from Panjab University and subsequently her M.Tech. in Computer Science in 2017 from Punjab Engineering College, Chandigarh. Dr. Ridhma has submitted her PhD thesis at Punjab Engineering College, Chandigarh. Her research interest areas include deep learning, machine learning, computer vision and image processing. She has taken labs of various courses during her PhD tenure at Punjab Engineering College, Chandigarh.
