
    Q: What is the objective of the B.Tech in Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence at JKLU?
    A: The program provides a strong foundation in AI and Machine Learning, along with computer science fundamentals, with exposure to cutting-edge AI technologies like NLP, Computer Vision, and Reinforcement Learning.

    Q: What are the career prospects of the B.Tech in Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence?
    A: Graduates are prepared for careers in applying AI to healthcare, analytics, fintech, transportation, and e-commerce.
