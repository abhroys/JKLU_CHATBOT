

# Stepper Squad (Dance Club) #


# About Club #

Welcome to Steppers Squad, a haven where every step unfolds a unique story, and every beat resonates with rhythm and soul. More than just a dance club, Steppers Squad is a place where passionate individuals are brought together by a shared love for music and dance. Join us in this rhythmic journey, where the dance floor is our canvas to create melodies and memories of a lifetime.


# Our Mission #

At Steppers Squad, our mission is to inspire, empower, and elevate through dance. We believe in the transformative power of the beautiful art form of dance as a means of self-expression, communication, and personal growth. Our aim to foster creativity, teamwork, and confidence in our members celebrates the joy of dance.

With every performance, we transcend the ordinary and enter a world where music becomes motion. Join us on this mesmerising journey as we choreograph memories that linger long after the music fades.


# What We Offer #

– Dance Education

Steppers Squad offers a diverse range of dance styles and levels, from contemporary to hip-hop, catering to both beginners and seasoned dancers.

– Performance Opportunities

As a member of Steppers Squad, get a chance to showcase your talent in various performances, competitions, and events. We believe that dance is meant to be shared, and our stages provide the perfect platform to do so.

– Community and Support

Beyond dance, we are a tight-knit community that supports one another. We believe in the power of unity and camaraderie, fostering lifelong friendships and a sense of belonging.

– Personal Growth

Stepping into the spotlight with Steppers Squad means embracing your inner dancer and unlocking your potential. Our club is not just about dance; it’s about personal growth, self-confidence, and self- discovery.

Become a part of the Steppers Squad legacy, where rhythm meets passion, and dance becomes a journey of self-expression and empowerment.

# Club Rules and Regulations #
- The club members may suggest a new activity or take part in/host an existing activity.
- All club members must agree to attend club meetings.
- Evaluation must be conducted in an appropriate manner.
- Even in on-campus mode, the contest will be done online.
- Only the teams who meet the eligibility criteria will be registered and permitted to take part in any competition.
- No team or individual participant in more than one team may register twice.
- During the contest term, each participating team must submit solutions.
- Sharing duplicate submissions and codes is prohibited, and any such incident will result in disqualification.
