
    Q: What is the Community Development Club (CDC) at JKLU?
    A: The Community Development Club focuses on sensitizing students to their responsibilities as citizens and encouraging them to work collaboratively toward sustainable development goals and positive social change.

    Q: What activities does the Community Development Club offer?
    A: The club organizes social gatherings, informative sessions, and community service events to promote awareness of social issues and encourage action towards solutions.
