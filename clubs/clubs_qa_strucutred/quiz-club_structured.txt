
    Q: What is Inquiztors (Quiz Club) at JKLU?
    A: Inquiztors is JKLU’s quiz club that organizes quizzes to test students’ mental ability and enhance their knowledge and confidence, preparing them for competitive examinations and building their social awareness.

    Q: What is the mission of Inquiztors (Quiz Club)?
    A: The mission is to prepare students for challenges requiring broad knowledge, create awareness, and help students excel in academic and general knowledge competitions.
