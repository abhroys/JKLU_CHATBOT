
    Q: What is The Shade (Photography Club) at JKLU?
    A: The Shade is a community of photography enthusiasts at JKLU that explores the nuances of photography and cinematography through workshops, mentorship, and photography outings. The club helps students enhance their technical skills and creativity.

    Q: What activities does The Shade (Photography Club) offer?
    A: The club offers skill development workshops, photo outings, exhibitions, networking opportunities, access to photography equipment, and photography competitions.
