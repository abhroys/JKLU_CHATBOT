

# The Shade (Photography Club) #


# About the club #

The Shade, JKLU Photography Club is a vibrant community of photography enthusiasts who share a passion for capturing the world through their lenses. Our club provides a creative platform for members to explore the interplay of light and shadow in their photographic pursuits. Regular meetings, workshops, and photo outings organised by the club aim to sharpen technical skills, exchange ideas, and foster a deeper appreciation for the art of photography. Whether you’re a novice or a seasoned professional, The Shade welcomes all who seek to master the nuances of shading, contrast, and composition in the world of imagery.


# Vision #

To landmark photography and cinematography more than just a hobby and establish a sustainable and supportive environment for photography enthusiast of all level.


# Mission #

To provide a friendly and supportive environment for all photographers across the university, encouraging the growth of creativity & technical knowledge and to explore the connection across the lens.


# What We Offer #

– Skill Development

Access to workshops, tutorials, and mentorship to enhance photography skills, from basics to advanced techniques.

– Community

Join a supportive and like-minded community of photographers, share your work, and receive constructive feedback.

– Inspiration

Regular photo outings and exhibitions to spark your creativity and broaden horizons.

– Networking

Connect with fellow photographers, collaborate on projects, and build valuable relationships in the photography industry.

– Access to Resources

Exclusive access to photography equipment, studios, and editing tools.

– Educational Events

Guest speakers and experts in the field provide insights and knowledge on various photography genres.

– Competitions

Participate in club-sponsored photography contests and challenges to showcase your talent.

– Social Engagement

Engage in photography-related discussions, share your experiences, and make lasting friendships with fellow photographers.

# Club Rules and Regulations #
- The club members may suggest a new activity or take part in/host an existing activity.
- All club members must agree to attend club meetings.
- Evaluation must be conducted in an appropriate manner.
- Even in on-campus mode, the contest will be done online.
