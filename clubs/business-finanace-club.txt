

# CORPOVA (Business and Finance Club) #


# About Club #

The Corpova Business Club is a dynamic and exclusive community of forward-thinking entrepreneurs, executives, and business professionals who share a common passion for innovation, growth, and success. Our club is a hub for networking, knowledge sharing, and fostering meaningful connections within the business world.


# Our Mission #

The Corpova Business Club is more than just a networking group; it’s a platform for those who are passionate about making a significant impact in the business world. We invite you to become a part of our thriving community and embark on a journey of growth, collaboration, and success. We aim to empower members with the resources, insights, and collaborations necessary to excel in the ever-evolving landscape of business.


# What We Offer #

– Networking Opportunities

The Corpova Business Club hosts regular networking events, seminars, and workshops where members can connect with industry leaders and like-minded individuals.

– Knowledge Sharing

Our club provides access to exclusive business insights, market trends, and thought leadership through guest speakers, panel discussions, and educational sessions.

– Mentorship Programs

We offer mentorship opportunities that pair experienced business leaders with emerging talent, thereby fostering personal and professional growth.

# Common Rules and regulations: #
- All members have to attend all the meetings.
- Members have to participate in maximum activities of the club.
- Have to bring in maximum enthusiasm, cheerfulness and energetic vibes.
- Respect dignity of each member and Jury members.
- All proposed club activities must be reviewed and approved by the President or Vice President of Student Life before a club member may present it for approval to the faculty co-ordinator.

# Club Rules and Regulations #
- The club members may suggest a new activity or take part in/host an existing activity.
- All club members must agree to attend club meetings.
- Evaluation must be conducted in an appropriate manner.
- Even in on-campus mode, the contest will be done online.
- Only the teams who meet the eligibility criteria will be registered and permitted to take part in any competition.
- No team or individual participant in more than one team may register twice.
- During the contest term, each participating team must submit solutions.
- Sharing duplicate submissions and codes is prohibited, and any such incident will result in disqualification.
