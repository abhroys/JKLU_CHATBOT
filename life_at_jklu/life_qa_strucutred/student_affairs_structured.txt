
    Q: What is the role of the Department of Student Affairs at JKLU?
    A: The Department of Student Affairs supports students' well-being and fosters personal development through cultural and sports activities, managing 15 Development Clubs, hostel facilities, healthcare, and organizing national-level events like Sabrang and Spardha.

    Q: What services does the Department of Student Affairs provide?
    A: It manages the smooth functioning of the hostel, mess, transport, fitness center, healthcare, and student welfare committees, and collaborates with Mindery for counseling services.
