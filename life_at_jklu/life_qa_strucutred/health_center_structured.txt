
    Q: What healthcare facilities are available at JKLU?
    A: JKLU has a team of qualified doctors and healthcare staff at the university medical center, providing round-the-clock medical assistance. A 24x7 ambulance service is also available for emergencies.

    Q: What counseling services are offered at JKLU?
    A: The 'Mindery' on-campus counseling service provides students with 24x7 online counseling and in-person sessions every Thursday, helping students manage anxiety and adjust to campus life.
